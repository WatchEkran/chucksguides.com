C - HOW TO RECEIVE MESSAGES WITH SADL
1. NEW MSG means that a new message has been received.
2. Select MSG Page
3. Select RCVD.
4. Message will be displayed.
5. To acknowledge the message, click on ACK.
6. To delete the message, click DEL.
